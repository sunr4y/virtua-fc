Live Match

Transfers & Scouting

Send scouts, negotiate with counter-offers, loan players in and out, or sign pre-contracts with free agents.

Youth Academy

New prospects every season with hidden attributes. Decide who earns a first-team spot, goes on loan, or gets released.
